Apply for Moderator Position

Interested in becoming a moderator for our server management team? Join our Discord server and head to the Apply Moderator channel to submit your application!

How to Apply

  1. Join our Discord server.
  2. Navigate to the work-4-us channel.
  3. Follow the instructions provided in the channel to submit your application.

Requirements

  • Proven experience in server management and moderation.
  • Familiarity with ticketing systems and server moderation tools.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to handle and resolve conflicts effectively.
  • Dedication to maintaining a positive and inclusive community environment.

Responsibilities

  • Running and managing tickets efficiently.
  • Moderating servers to ensure a safe and welcoming community.
  • Resolving conflicts and addressing user concerns.
  • Collaborating with other moderators to uphold community standards.

Compensation

Moderators will receive a monthly payment depending on the type of work and their dedicated efforts in maintaining a positive server environment. Performance reviews may lead to adjustments in compensation based on the quality of moderation and contributions to the community.

Thank You for considering the opportunity to join our moderation team! We appreciate your interest and look forward to reviewing your application.
